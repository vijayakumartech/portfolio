Admissions

Admissions


Parents are advised to call each school for seat availability. Admission forms for all branches of The Foundation School are the same and are available below throughout the year. Parents can download the forms, fill them, and submit them at the relevant branch of the school where they are seeking admission for the child. Parents also need to submit the set of documents listed below along with the admission forms. The final decisions on admissions will be taken after April 11th. In case of any queries or clarifications, more information can be obtained at the relevant school. Please visit the "Contact Us" page for phone numbers and addresses.

ADMISSION FORM (click below to download):

Admission Form Download


Documents to be submitted along with the admission forms:

  1. Admission Form
  2. Birth Certificate
  3. Four passport size photographs
  4. Previous year's report card/transcript (if applicable)
